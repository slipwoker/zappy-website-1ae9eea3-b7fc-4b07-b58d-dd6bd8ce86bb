How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 4–6 weeks ahead for weekends, though we can accommodate last-minute requests when available.

Do you provide sound equipment?

We bring professional DJ gear and can coordinate full sound systems for an additional fee depending on venue size.

Absolutely. We craft bespoke sets that match your event's energy, from corporate receptions to club nights.

What's your typical set length?

Standard bookings are 2–4 hours, with flexibility for extended sessions or multiple sets throughout the night.

Do you take song requests during events?

Yes, we read the room and incorporate requests that fit the flow—keeping the dance floor energized is our priority.
